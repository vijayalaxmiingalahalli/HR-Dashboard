# HR Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard built in Power BI to analyze employee attrition, salary trends, job satisfaction, departmental performance, and workforce demographics. The dashboard helps HR teams identify key factors affecting employee retention and supports data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze employee attrition across different departments and job roles.
- Monitor key HR metrics using interactive KPI cards.
- Identify trends in employee salary, satisfaction, and work-life balance.
- Provide insights to improve employee retention and organizational performance.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Microsoft Excel

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### KPI Cards
- Total Employees
- Attrition Count
- Average Salary
- Average Satisfaction

### Interactive Filters
- Department
- Gender
- Job Role

### Visualizations
- Attrition Count by Department
- Attrition Count by Job Role
- Attrition Count by Monthly Income
- Attrition Count by Hourly Rate
- Attrition Count by Years at Company
- Attrition Count by Job Satisfaction
- Attrition Count by Work-Life Balance
- Average Salary by Department
- Employee Distribution by Monthly Income
- Performance Rating vs Monthly Income

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Research & Development department has the highest employee attrition.
- Employees with lower monthly income show higher attrition.
- Job satisfaction and work-life balance have a significant impact on employee retention.
- Salary distribution varies across departments.
- Most employees fall within the lower monthly income range.
